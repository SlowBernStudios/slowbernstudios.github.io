About Us

Slowbern Studios creates games that simmer — rich with imagination, emotion, and playful depth. We’re a small, independent team with a passion for meaningful experiences that leave a lasting spark.

Our Story

Founded on the idea that not everything needs to move fast to make an impact, Slowbern Studios builds stylized, story-driven games for players who like to think, laugh, and explore at their own pace. Our work blends immersive worlds, subtle humor, and thoughtful mechanics — from retail sims about selling water to fish, to underwater journeys of conservation, to color-driven prison escapes. We believe in crafting games that are more than just fun — they’re experiences to sit with, to savor, and to remember.
